Who We Are

Your Trusted
Travel Partner

Trip Fly BD began its journey in February 2023 with a clear mission — to make international and domestic travel simple, affordable, and stress-free for every Bangladeshi dreamer who wants to explore the world.

From our very first day, we focused on building trust. Every client — whether planning a Thailand honeymoon, a family trip to India, a solo medical visit abroad, or simply needing a domestic air ticket to Cox’s Bazar — receives the same level of dedicated, expert attention.

Today, Trip Fly BD is recognised as one of Bangladesh's fastest-growing air travel agencies. We handle tourist visas, student visas, group visas, medical visas, international and domestic air ticketing, hotel bookings, and fully guided tour packages. Our consultants understand the exact requirements of over 30 international embassies and airlines.

Our Vision

Our Journey

From Day One to Today

A young agency built on trust, hard work and genuine passion for travel

February 2023

Trip Fly BD Founded

We opened our doors with a small but passionate team, determined to simplify travel and visa services for Bangladeshi clients. From day one, WhatsApp-first customer service set us apart.

Mid 2023

First 100 Visa Approvals

Within months, we processed our first 100 successful tourist and group visa applications across Thailand, Malaysia, India and Dubai — building the foundation of our 99% success rate.

Late 2023

Domestic Air Ticketing Launched

We expanded to include domestic air ticket booking for all Bangladesh routes — Dhaka–Cox’s Bazar, Dhaka–Sylhet, Dhaka–Chittagong and all other available domestic routes.

2024

Couple & Group Tour Packages

Launched premium couple honeymoon packages and group tour programmes covering Thailand, Maldives, Malaysia, Singapore and more — all-inclusive with hotels, transfers and visa included.

2025

2,000+ Happy Clients & Growing

Trip Fly BD now serves over 2,000 satisfied clients, covers 30+ international destinations, and continues to grow as Bangladesh’s fastest-rising travel agency — with you as our number one priority.
